Manuel


1. Movement


Use the WASD keys to move the player around the board. This can help to evade enemies. If you have the speed upgrade, your player will move faster.

2. Attacking


Left click to attack, this will fire a swirl at the mouse's location. If you have the tracking upgrade, the swirls will track the enemy up to a certain radius. These swirls will have varing damage depending on the damage upgrade level.

3. Coins


Coins will drop when an enmemy is killed. You can collect them by running over them with your player. If you have the magnet upgrade, coins will follow you up to a certain radius.

4. Health


Health will naturally regenerate over time if you have the regen upgrade. If you have the health upgrade, you will be granted extra max health. Health packs will give you ten health points each, collect them by running over them with your player.

5. Enemies


If a normal enemy uses their attack on you, you will lose damage rapidly especially if you have the enemy upgrade. The enemy upgrade upgrades all aspects of the enemies. If you have the enemy type upgrade, you will be granted a different enemy type. This enemy type will have a different attack pattern to challenge your player.

6. Utilities


The ESC button will pause and unpause the game. This will help you to take a break from the game or contemplate your 1hp. The kills counter on the top left shows how many enemies you have killed in that session. The coins counter on the top left shows how many coins you have collected in that session.
